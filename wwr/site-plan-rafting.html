Purpose

Whether you are a first timer or seasoned river veteran, our guides and staff are there to make sure you have the trip of a lifetime. From falling asleep beneath the stars at night to enjoying the refreshing splash of rapids on a hot day, we will take care of the details and leave you free to explore.

Audience

We specialize in family trips, focused on amazing adventures, healthy food, and creating good memories. Our staff and guides are excited that you have picked us to join you for your vacation on the river.
