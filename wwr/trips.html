Trips we offer:

Boise River:

The Boise River is lined with beautiful black cottonwood trees and wildlife can be seen like blue heron, osprey, red tail hawks, king fisher, and beaver. This trip is Class 1-2 and is for those that want a nice and relaxed float while experiencing Boise from a unique perspective.

Payette River:

Whether you find yourself in McCall or Boise if you are looking for a a fun day on the river for your group of friends or family the Cabarton section of the Payette River is very possibly what you are looking for.

Salmon River:

For any family looking for one of the premier river adventures in the U.S. the Main Salmon should be on the list. Big splashy class III and IV rapids, mixed with smaller whitewater and placid pools mean this trip has all the excitement one could want without having too much.
